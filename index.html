    The Trade Workers Protection Plan is a program specifically designed to meet the needs of trade workers in Canada.

Our goal is to provide you with the opportunity to fill in those missing gaps. So that you and your family will be properly protected and taken care of in case you face an occupational hazard. It is important to note that this plan is not intended to replace your current coverage, rather to enhance it.

    Exclusive Benefits


    As a member of the Trade Workers Protection Plan you have access to many exclusive benefits designed specifically for you.



    Your exclusive benefits may include:



    Emergency Assistance Program

    Emergency grants are available to provide short term, temporary financial assistance to members affected by significant personal hardship, disaster or large scale emergency.

    Orphan Benefits

    A monthly payment for orphans, which pays $900 per child to the legal guardian for children under age 18 upon death of the parents.

    Orphan Scholarships

    An annual payment of up to $6,000 per year for up to 4 years for each child, to cover tuition and maintenance for higher learning.

    Post Secondary Education Scholarship

    250 scholarships are granted each year for higher learning (up to $8,000). Students can apply with a minimum grade point average of 70% and 40 hours of community service.

    Tutoring Services

    90 hours of tutoring is available if your child misses more than 2 weeks of school.

    Post Hospitalization Benefit

    After 2 days or more in hospital you have access to assistance with housekeeping, home medication delivery, health monitoring system and transportation.

    Childbirth Benefit

    After giving birth you can receive support of a registered nurse and other services for up to 3 days.

    Legal Services

    Access to free and/or discounted legal services with a lawyer in your area for help with a variety of issues including wills, family law and home ownership, etc.

    Everyday Financial Helpline

    Phone assistance with an accredited counsellor that can help you manage your money.

    Terminal Illness Loan

    If the member becomes terminally ill, they are eligible to receive up to 75% of the total life insurance plan up to $250,000 interest free.Bereavement Assistance Beneficiaries can be reimbursed up to $1,000 for counselling services to help them deal with their loss.

    Consulting Services

    Expert and informed advice on health information including making specialist appointments and planning home care.

    Family Fun Events

    Members are invited to enjoy free and discounted fun events including theme parks, sporting events and more each year.

    Project Grants

    Members will have the opportunity to organize local volunteer projects that contribute to the well-being of families and communities with financial assistance.

    Volunteering

    Members have the opportunity to become involved in inspiring community activities such as park builds and raising money for different charities.

    Health Assistance

    Access to advice on different medical conditions and situations by doctors and registered nurses.

    Member Discounts on Products and Services

    Including Medical, Dental, Vision, Hearing, Nursing, Massage, Physio, Chiropractic, Medical Equipment, Fitness, Wellness, Shopping, Entertainment and Restaurants.
